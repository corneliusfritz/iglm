# A network of friendships between students at Rice University.

The data was collected by Facebook and provided as part of Traud et al.
(2012)

## Format

This data object is a pre-computed \`netplus\` object. It models the
\`rice\` friendship network (\`z_network\`) using two binary covariates:
gender (\`x_attribute\`) and whether the graduation year is 2008
(\`y_attribute\`). The "neighborhood" structure (\`neighborhood\`) is
defined as students sharing the same dormitory. data(rice)

## References

Traud, Mucha, Porter (2012). Social Structure of Facebook Network.
Physica A: Statistical Mechanics and its Applications, 391, 4165-4180
